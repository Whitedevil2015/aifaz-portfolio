Core Islamic Values

The Pillars of Character

Sidq (Truthfulness)

Speaking the truth in every situation, for truth leads to righteousness and Paradise.

Amanah (Honesty)

Fulfilling trusts and responsibilities with absolute integrity and reliability.

Ihsan (Excellence)

Worshipping Allah as if you see Him, and striving for perfection in all deeds.

Sabr (Patience)

Enduring hardships with dignity and unwavering faith in Allah's decree.

Shukr (Gratitude)

Acknowledging blessings and using them in obedience to the Bestower.

Tawadu (Humility)

Lowering the wing of mercy to others and avoiding arrogance and pride.

Adl (Justice)

Upholding fairness and equity, even if it is against oneself.

Rahmah (Compassion)

Showing mercy to all creation, for those who show mercy will be shown mercy.

Islamic Adab & Manners

Conduct of a Believer

Family Relations

Kindness to parents, strengthening ties of kinship, and harmonious home life.

Social Conduct

Greeting with Salam, honoring guests, and respecting the rights of neighbors.

Business Ethics

Honest dealings, fair measurements, and fulfilling contracts without deceit.

Personal Development

Continuous seeking of knowledge, purification of the soul (Tazkiyah), and self-discipline.
